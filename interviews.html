A Short AutoBiography With Our Founder
Martino Henderson

Hello, my name is Martino Sinatra Henderson, Sr. and this is me. I grew up in Chicago, Illinois where I was raised by my working class Mother and Step-Father. I never got to know my biological father and never really got to miss him either. My step-father has been there for me since I was one year old, in other words before I could remember. I was raised in a house full of adults and there were not many children in the neighborhood where I lived. My brother was not born until after I turned five years old. In short I grew up very mature for my age and learned to be independent very early. My parents allowed me to be free to explore my curiosities without the constant overview or tether to their hips.

About DigitalXIII.com

Your Technology Resource

Site Purpose:

DigitalXIII.com is an online only webzine dedicated to both consumer and business technology news, current trends, and product reviews. Our site will have live coverage of technology related conferences, product announcements, consumer product expositions. We will bring you the latest business news that effects technology professionals. Our contributors will conduct interviews with CIOs and other System administrators about the latest trends in business systems, security, mobile and computer technology within our “Pro Center”. For consumers we will feature in-depth and comprehensive reviews of the latest mobile devices, gadgets, computers, entertainment, and other technology. Don’t forget to check out our weekly tutorials, tips, tricks, and trade secrets straight from the pros.

Target Audience:

DigitalXIII.com will primarily focus on people searching for specific, in-depth, information and a comprehensive review on consumer technology and gadgets such as smartphones, computers, home entertainment. As well as technology professionals who will subscribe to our RSS feed for coverage of technology events and interviews with other technology professionals who are willing to share information about deploying new systems that could help our customers. The most likely age range to be attracted to DigitalXIII.com will be 18 - 35 year olds who are searching for information on specific products. We plan to target an audience of 20 – 45 year olds who work in the technology industry with regular interviews with other professionals as well as reviews of business system hardware, software, and solutions.

Message:

We plan to attract a dedicated audience of technology professionals by featuring coverage of trade shows, technology conferences, and consumer expos. Our contributors will build relationships with businesses in order to gain exclusive announcements of new products. We plan to become the single source on the web for exclusive introductions, interviews, and announcements of everything happening within technology. Setting our site apart from others with similar goals will be from interviews with technology professionals who manage or have deployed new business systems by showcasing new trends in the industry. We will also offer tips, tricks, tutorials, techniques, trade secrets, and how to-s that can range in levels from beginner to experienced. We plan to keep the webzine upscale and focused on the technology professional who would like to gain an inside track on what is happening within the industry by developing our site as like the Esquire for technology professionals.
